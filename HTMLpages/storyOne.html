The Partial Tales of Heracles

Heracles had a cousin, Eurystheus, the king of Tiryns. Some rumors say that Eurystheus imposed 12 Labours onto Heracles to atone for sins, but other rumors say it was familial rivalry. No one knows for sure.

You, reader, are going to help Heracles overcome some of those trials!

The first trial that Eurystheus gave to Heracles was to defeat the Nemean Lion. The Nemean lion is said to have impenetrable fur...

You, as Heracles, set out to find a way to defeat the legendary Nemean Lion.
